# HD-Designprompts der ersten Rookie-Monster

Gemeinsamer Stilblock für PixelLab oder spätere neue Varianten:

```text
Polished high-definition 2D anime-inspired cel-shaded game illustration, smooth clean anti-aliased curves, controlled line weight, strong readable silhouette, subtle two-step shading, professional browser and mobile game character sprite. Absolutely no pixel blocks, no pixel-art texture, no retro rendering. Exactly one original creature in a calm neutral idle pose suitable for later skeletal or frame animation. Keep limbs clearly separated and avoid extreme foreshortening. Full body strict side view, centered, generous transparent padding, no cropping, no cast shadow, no text, no UI, no logo, no watermark, no resemblance to an existing franchise character. Final runtime canvas exactly 200x200 pixels.
```

## Pyrook

```text
Small brave young fire bird-dragon rookie monster, coral fire-feathers, cream face and belly, charcoal beak and feet, huge expressive amber eye, short flame-shaped feather horns, readable near wing, glowing orange diamond core embedded in the chest, cute and companionable but battle-ready, facing right. Palette: coral, burnt orange, warm cream, amber and charcoal.
```

## Mossbit

```text
Small calm young forest guardian rookie monster, low sturdy quadruped silhouette, cream face, oversized mint eye, layered moss-and-leaf armor shell with controlled large leaf shapes, bark-textured legs, two short branch horns with fresh buds, geometric pale mint core framed by bark on the shoulder, cute and protective, facing left. Palette: moss green, deep teal, bark brown, pale mint and warm ivory.
```

## Voltfin

```text
Small alert young lightning-water rookie monster, fast amphibious lizard-fish silhouette, deep navy body, oversized cyan eye, opaque pale-cyan tail fin, lightning-shaped ear fins, small webbed feet, electric yellow markings, pale geometric chest core, playful and built for speed, facing right. Palette: electric yellow, pale cyan, deep navy, warm ivory and charcoal.
```

Für eine PixelLab-Animation bleibt der Designblock unverändert. Ergänzt werden nur Animation, Richtung und Frameplan; Identität, Proportionen, Palette und 200×200-Leinwand müssen gesperrt bleiben.
